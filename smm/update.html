You can now play in multiplayer!
To do this, right click in-game on Firefox and point to "Add player"!
(If you don't have Firefox, add "&luigi" and/or "&peach" in the address bar, this will bring up the characters ...)

To switch the camera between multiple players, use T to go to the previous player or Y to go to the next player!
Vertical camera added

The controls for Mario are as follows:
Ctrl - Running / Throwing a fireball
↑ - Jump
← - Go left
↓ - Crouch
→ - Go right

The controls for Luigi are as follows:
A - Running / Throwing a fireball
Z - Jump
Q - Go left
S - Crouch
D - Go right

The controls for Peach are as follows:
N - Running / Throwing a fireball
I - Jump
J - Go left
K - Crouch
L - Go right
